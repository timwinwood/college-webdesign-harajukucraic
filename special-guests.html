Special Guests

Get to know them!

Angel Cosplay

Rachel Lloyds (AKA Angel Cosplay) is an acclaimed costume designer, author and cosplayer from Germany. With over 10 years of experience and a neverending desire to start the next big project she has already created many award winning costumes and lately published a hugely popular series of cosplay instructional books that have become the entry point for many Cosplay newcomers worldwide.

Even though she now tours all over the world at international conventions she loves nothing more than to support newcomers as well as veterans in creating their big dream costumes.

Jen Anime

Jen Anime is an otaku/anime Youtuber with over 1.9 million subscribers. Jen’s content mainly revolves around otaku fandom and anime culture- from reviews, parodies, skits, and rants.

She has also included Japanese culture/vlog content since her recent move to Japan in January 2019.

Since the creation of her channel in 2014, Jen has committed to sharing her loveand appreciation for anime and otaku culture with viewers all over the world.

Tiny Wing Cosplay

Coming from Northern Ireland, Jordan has been cosplaying for over 10 years. She started making her own cosplays just 4 years ago and have caught the attention of many. With gaming and anime as a huge part of her childhood, Jordan’s cosplays mainly focus on gaming related characters. She doesn’t like to follow ‘Trends’ and insteads go backwards and like to cosplay as the classics. She is known for her mythical cosplays.

She mainly crafts using fabric, eva foam and also incorporates 3d printing into her builds. Every part of her designs have so much care and attention given which stands her creations out from the crowd.
